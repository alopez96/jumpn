Our Vision

We want every student to make the best informed decision when applying to college. Traditionally, the best way to get this information has been to visit campuses and go on tours, talk to school staff and visit the city where the school is.
Deciding which school to go is one the biggest decisions a student can make. But students and parents might not have the time or money to make a trip to visit these colleges.
We want to help students make the best informed decision when applying and searching for colleges. You will get tips and ratings on schools, as well as get the chance to ask questions and connect with other students.

Why This Exists

Message from Founder: When I was applying to schools, I visited over 60 websites to check out resources, academics, engineering program, town, and more. School websites gave me outdated information that was hard to find, and it always felt like I was visiting a site that said "come to our school because we are the best."

I created this platform, for users to get opinions from different parties. Asking questions and gaining as much knowledge about a school or city can help you make this big decision. Use this platform to connect with other students, just read some tips, or ask your specific questions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I have to do to become a publisher?

The users that are given access to post articles about a specific school will need to update profile to describe their affiliation with the school.

Am I allowed to use emojis on this site?

You can use all the emojis you want to make your point, make sure you have fun with it and if someone likes your emojis, you get points.

When will the site launch?

We will be making an announcement about our public launch once we're ready. An anticipated date will be posted on here when we have one. Also subscribe to get up to date information.

Will all the features, including the blog and chat, be available at lauch day?

Yes, every service we described will be ready for users at lauch day.

Will there be a cost for using the platform?

There will be two user subscription options. The first will be completely free, and will give you access to everything but the professional advice from our team. You will get to see and post reviews, tips, and also get access to the chats.

The paid subscription will be for users who want to get help with resume advice, college application support, and financial aid application assisstance.
